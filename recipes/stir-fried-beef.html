Stir fried beef with broccoli

Ingredients

  • Skirt steak sliced into thin pieces
  • Light soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Shaoxing wine
  • MSG
  • Cornstarch
  • Baking soda
  • Kosher salt
  • Sugar
  • Oyster sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Broccoli
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Peanut oil

Steps

  1. Marinade beef and massage the meat for at least 30 seconds
  2. To make the sauce, combine soy sauce, wine, vinegar, oyster sauce and water
  3. Heat up water in a wok and after it boils, add the broccoli and cook until tender-crisp
  4. Heat the wok over high heat until lightly smoking, then add the beef and cook until lightly seared
    • Add garlic and ginger and stir-fry until fragrant
    • Add Shaoxing wine, sauce mixture and constarch slurry to the wok
  5. Serve immediately with white steamed rice
