Chicken noodle soup recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Diced carrots
  • Diced celery
  • Salt and pepper
  • Melted chicken fat
  • Roasted chicken broth
  • Uncooked egg noodles
  • Cayenne pepper
  1. Melt butter in a large pot with the vegetables and cook until soft
  2. Stir in roasted chicken broth and bring to a boil
  3. Stir in egg noodles and chicken fat
  4. Add cayenne pepper and season to taste with salt and pepper
  5. Serve while hot
